Katherine Olivia Sessions

Mother of Balboa Park

A brief timeline of Katherine Session's life:

  • 1857 - Born on Nob Hill in San Francisco
  • 1881 - Became the first woman to gradiate with a B.S. from the University of California, Berkeley
  • 1884 - Moved to San Diego to work as a teacher at Russ School
  • 1885 - Purchased the San Diego Nursery with her friends Mr. and Mrs. Solon Blasidell
  • 1892 - Leased land in what was then called "City Park" for a nursery in exchange for planting 100 trees a year in the park and furnishing 300 more for planting throughout the city
  • 1902 - Was instrumental in the formation of the Park Improvement Committee with her friends George Marston and Mary B. Coulson
  • 1906 - Helped found the San Diego Floral Association
  • 1915 - Appointed the supervisor of agriculture and landscaper for the city schools
  • 1939 - The first woman awared the Frank N. Meyer medal of the American Genetic Association
  • 1940 - Died at the age of 83
